Payment details
Please provide payment information so we can set up your direct debit before admission.
Direct debit setup
Fees are charged 4-weekly in arrears by direct debit. Your bank details will be used to set this up before your admission date.
Your bank details are collected for direct debit setup only. They will be held securely by JDRH Care in line with their data protection policy.
Local authority payment
As this is a local authority placement, your contribution will be assessed and collected by Aberdeenshire Council. Your care manager will advise you of the collection arrangements.
If you are required to pay an additional top-up amount directly, JDRH Care will contact you separately to arrange a direct debit for that portion.
